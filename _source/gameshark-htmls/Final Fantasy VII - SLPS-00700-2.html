Keep in mind that Aerith is in your party permanently, and that the game will crash if you have a conversation/group gathering with Aerith in your party after Disc 1. This code is far less buggy than the 'Character Select', but you should still turn the code off once Aerith in in your party or the game will crash. %NOTE
