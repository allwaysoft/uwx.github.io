Cool Boarders 2 - SCES-00992

Infinite Checkpoint Time

80091454 0000
You must combine the "Infinite Checkpoint Time" and "Always place first" for them to function properly. If you use "Infinite Checkpoint Time" and "Always place first" in Competition mode you should turn your codes off after finishing the last race. If you don't, the game has a tendency to crash during the credit scene. Also, using "Infinite Checkpoint Time" and "Always place first" will not stop the time in Trick Master Mode and will also screw the character graphics up.
