<pre>Syntax
     DBCC CHECKCATALOG 
        [('<i>database_name</i>' | <i>database_id</i> | 0 )]
           [WITH NO_INFOMSGS]

 Key:
   WITH NO_INFOMSGS - Suppresses all informational messages.
