<!-- #BeginLibraryItem "/Library/head_vb.lbi" --><!-- #EndLibraryItem --><h1>Space</h1> 
<p>Return  a string consisting of a specified number of spaces. </p>
<pre>Syntax 
      Space(<i>number</i>)

Key
   <i>number</i>    The number of spaces to add to the string.</pre>
<p>Example</p>
<pre>wscript.echo "Hello" &amp; Space(10) &amp; "World" ' Insert 10 spaces between two strings.</pre>
