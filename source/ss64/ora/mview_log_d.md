<!-- #BeginLibraryItem "/Library/head_ora.lbi" --><!-- #EndLibraryItem --><h1>DROP MATERIALIZED VIEW LOG</h1> 
<p>Drop a materialized view log. The word "snapshot" is synonymous 
  with "materialized view". <br>
  <br>
  Syntax:</p>
<pre>   DROP MATERIALIZED VIEW LOG ON [<i>schema</i>.]<i>table</i>;
 
</pre>
